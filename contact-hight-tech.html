Common correspondences

We ship to thirty-eight countries. Standard delivery is three to five working days within Europe; seven to twelve internationally. All orders are dispatched from our Paris fulfilment house within one working day of placement.
Thursday and Friday between 10:00 and 18:00, and Saturday from 11:00, require no appointment. For private consultations with our formulation team — which we recommend for first visits — please write to us in advance.
Unopened products may be returned within twenty-one days of receipt. We do not accept returns of opened formulations for hygiene reasons, however if you are dissatisfied with the performance of any product, please contact us and we will find a resolution.
Most of our formulas are appropriate for reactive and sensitised skin. Each product page lists known sensitisers clearly. For specific dermatological questions we recommend corresponding with our formulation team before purchase.
We offer a bespoke consultation service for a limited number of clients each year. The process involves a detailed skin assessment and correspondence with our lead formulator. Inquire via the form above selecting "Formulation" as the subject.
Every ingredient is sourced directly from cultivators or wildcrafters with whom we have long-standing relationships. We do not purchase from distributors. Full ingredient provenance is available on request for each product in our current collection.
