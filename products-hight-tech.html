The Philosophy

Craft is not
a feature

We do not formulate for the shelf. Every ingredient is sourced at peak yield, every concentration is determined by efficacy — not margin. Our batches are small because quality cannot be scaled without being diluted.

The Vault archive contains sixty-four formulas developed since 2011. Twelve are in current production. The rest are held, awaiting materials of sufficient quality to proceed.
